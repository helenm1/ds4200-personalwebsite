About Solidcore

Solidcore is a high-intensity, low-impact workout that focuses on slow and controlled movements to build strength and endurance. It follows the Lagree method, a form of Pilates performed on a reformer-like machine called the "Sweatlana." Unlike traditional Pilates, Solidcore emphasizes time under tension, meaning every movement is slow and controlled to keep muscles engaged for longer. This results in a challenging, full-body workout that improves muscle tone, endurance, and core stability.
